Take More Control of Your Energy Bill

Shift & Save Pilot Program

Welcome to Shift & Save - a new way of pricing energy that puts YOU more in control.

Shift some of your electricity use to times during the day when electricity rates are lower – anytime outside 3 p.m. to 7 p.m. weekdays – and you will reduce your energy costs.

Get Started

Choose the Hours You Use Power

You make the choices that best fit your household and your budget. Shift & Save encourages you to take advantage of lower rate periods during weekdays. A few small changes can make a difference. When you run your dishwasher, clothes dryer, and clothes washer before 3 p.m. and after 7 p.m., for example, you’ll pay less.

Shift from Peak Hours for a lower rate.

Peak Hours are 3 p.m. to 7 p.m., Monday–Friday, year-round.

Shift & Save puts you in control when you know about Peak Hours and Off-Peak Hours.

• Peak Hours – 3:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. every weekday – when electricity rates and electricity demand are at their highest.

• Off-Peak Hours - 20 hours every weekday and all weekend hours with a lower electricity rate when you participate in Shift & Save.

What’s in it for you?

Simple changes in your schedule can lower what you pay.

Get free energy management tools to help track your usage.

Shift & Save is a pilot program designed with the State to help better manage demand on the grid, protect Michigan’s environment, and give you more control of your energy bill. There is no fee to join and no additional equipment is required.

How it works

Your electricity use is measured in kilowatts and you are charged a kilowatts-per-hour (kWh) rate. That rate is lower during Off-Peak hours. It’s also different in summer months (June through September) than it is the rest of the year (October through May), as you can see in the illustration below.

You can track your Shift & Save progress through your account and monthly bill. As shown on the right, your daily electric use is displayed in peak and off-peak hours. You can track month-to-month usage, too.

We’re here to help! Check out our welcome kit, energy savings tips, FAQs, and more, as we shift & save together.

Savings Tips

Appliances

Run your dishwasher, clothes dryer, and clothes washer before 3 p.m. and after 7 p.m. to take advantage of lower rates.

Heating/Cooling

Pre-cool your home before Peak Hours begin and raise the temperature by just four degrees during the peak period. You’ll keep cool and save.

Make a Habit of Saving

Unplug your coffee maker, microwave, and toaster when you’re not using them. They draw power just by being plugged in.
